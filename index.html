TIRUMALA

Tirumala, set atop the lush Tirumala hills of the Eastern Ghats, is not only a major spiritual hub due to the renowned Venkateswara Swamy Temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara but also a place of breathtaking natural beauty. As one of the world's richest and most visited religious sites, it holds immense historical significance with its Dravidian architectural marvels, intricate carvings, and a legacy that has been patronized by various rulers over centuries. Managed by the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams, the temple and its surroundings blend spiritual, historical, and natural allure seamlessly.
